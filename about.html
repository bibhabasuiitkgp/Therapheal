Our Lovely Team

Dr. Ananya Sinha
Founder, Director and Chief Clinical Psychologist

Dr. Ananya Sinha is an RCI-registered trauma-informed, LGBTQ-affirmative Clinical Psychologist with over 11 years of experience. She is an International Affiliate member of the American Psychological Association (APA) and a member of the Global Clinical Practice Network of World Health Organisation (WHO) and also a member of Society for Psychotherapy Research, Europe (EU) chapter.

Sailaja Menon
Global Expert

Sailaja Menon is a licensed Counselling Psychologist , with over 25 years of clinical experience in the field of Mental Health, Counselling, training, teaching and supervision.She has graduated as a Psychologist with a Post Master’s Degree (CAGS) specializing in Multicultural Counseling from the world-renowned and prestigious Johns Hopkins University in the United States
